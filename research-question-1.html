Research Focus

This research will investigate the influence of political and economic determinants—specifically the Democracy Index, Gender Inequality Index (GII), and GDP per capita—on the representation of female athletes in the upcoming Paris 2024 Olympics.

Through a thorough examination of these variables, the investigation aspires to elucidate the manner in which societal constructs dictate the opportunities afforded to women in the realm of sports.

Key Variables

  • Democracy Index
  • Gender Inequality Index (GII)
  • GDP per capita
