Research Question 2

Gender Equity in Sports Participation

Home Previous Question Next Question

Research Focus

This study will examine the correlation between gender equity in sports participation across various Olympic disciplines and the Gender Inequality Index (GII) of the respective nations.

The aim is to ascertain which sports demonstrate greater inclusivity and whether societal gender equality corresponds to enhanced participation equity in athletic endeavors.

Research Objectives

  • Analyze gender participation across Olympic sports
  • Correlate participation with national Gender Inequality Index
  • Identify most inclusive Olympic disciplines

Research Question 3

Economic Progress and Female Athlete Representation

Home Previous Question

Research Focus

This study will examine the patterns in the number of female athletes sent to the Olympics throughout time and how these changes correspond to the nations' economic progress, as defined by GDP.

The purpose is to determine how economic growth affects the progress of female athlete representation and if nations with greater GDP are more effective in achieving gender equality in sport.

Key Investigative Areas

  • Temporal trends in female athlete representation
  • Correlation between GDP and female athlete participation
  • Economic factors influencing gender equality in sports
